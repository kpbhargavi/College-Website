About Us

The college is dedicated to providing quality education in engineering and technology, focusing on empowering women to excel in various professional and leadership roles in the field. The goal is to foster a learning environment that promotes innovation, skill development, and holistic growth.

Why Choose Ashoka Women's Engineering College?

Ashoka Women's Engineering College is an excellent choice for those seeking quality education in a supportive and inclusive environment. The college is dedicated to empowering women in the field of engineering, offering a space where female students can thrive academically and professionally. With experienced faculty, modern classrooms, and state-of-the-art facilities, the college ensures that students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. It also focuses on skill development, preparing students for real-world challenges. Additionally, the positive atmosphere and strong industry connections provide students with good career opportunities, making it a great option for anyone looking to pursue engineering.

Located in NH 7, Opp Dupadu Railway Station, Lakshmipuram post, kurnool, Dupadu, Andhra Pradesh 518002.

Our Courses

Computer Science

Computer science engineering (CSE) is a field of study that involves the design, construction, and maintenance of computer systems and software. It's a broad discipline that includes the study of algorithms, data structures, and software development.

Civil Engineering

The role of a civil engineer is to design and implement structures that are used in everyday life such as bridges, roads and water supply systems. They are responsible for making sure these structures are created to a high standard and are safe.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineers design circuits that amplify signals, convert power from one form to another, or control the operation of a device. Power systems are another important topic that covers generation, transmission, and the distribution of electricity.
